Booking Policies

Cancellation Policy

If you need to cancel or reschedule your session, please do so at least 24 hours in advance. This allows me to offer the time to another client and ensures I can fully prepare for each session.

Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may not be eligible for a refund or reschedule.

Refund Policy

Due to the personalized and time-intensive nature of Human Design readings, all sales are final once a session has been completed. If you have concerns about your reading, please reach out to me directly at humandesign@hdcompass.com and I will do my best to address them.

Late Arrivals

If you arrive late to your session, we will use the remaining scheduled time. I am unable to extend sessions beyond the booked duration out of respect for other clients.

Compass AI Subscription

Compass AI subscriptions are billed monthly at $14 CAD. You may cancel at any time before your next billing date and will retain access until the end of the current billing period. No refunds are issued for partial months.

Questions?

If you have any questions about these policies, please don't hesitate to reach out at humandesign@hdcompass.com.
